🎯 The idea

Every team has a tier expectation — how far they're expected to go. You score points by beating that expectation, and lose points by falling short of it. The favourites aren't always the smart pick — sometimes the points are in backing an underdog, or betting against a big name.

📝 Your picks

1

Pick 3 teams. That's your portfolio for the tournament.

2

For each team, choose a position:

LONG 📈 — you back them to exceed their expectation.
SHORT 📉 — you back them to disappoint, finishing below their expectation.

3

Your total score = the points from all three picks added together. Highest score wins. 🥇

🏟️ The tiers

A team's tier sets its starting expectation (in points). The higher the tier, the more they're expected to achieve — so the harder they are to impress with.

📊 How points are scored

Each pick earns two kinds of points, added together.

1. Progression points. Every stage a team reaches is worth a progression score — the further they go, the higher it climbs:

Team reaches…Progression score
Group stage only0
Round of 328
Round of 1618
Quarter-Final32
Semi-Final48
Runner-up65
🏆 Champion85

Your points are that score measured against the team's expectation (its tier value — Serious Contenders 35, Dark Horses 15, Hopefuls 0):

LONG 📈 progression score − expectation
SHORT 📉 expectation − progression score

So a Long pick profits when the team goes further than expected, and a Short pick profits when they fall short of it.

2. Group-stage points — added on top, from the group games only. A Long pick wants results; a Short pick wants the opposite:

LONG 📈 +3 win   +1 draw   0 loss
SHORT 📉 +3 loss   +1 draw   0 win

💡 Worked examples

Going Long on a favourite — you pick Spain (Serious Contender, expectation 35) as LONG.
They win 2 and draw 1 in the group, then reach the Semi-Final (progression score 48). Progression: 48 − 35 = +13  ·  Group: (2×3)+(1×1) = +7  →  +20 pts
Shorting a big team that flops — you pick Germany (Serious Contender, expectation 35) as SHORT.
They win once and lose twice, crashing out in the group stage (progression score 0). Progression: 35 − 0 = +35  ·  Group: 2 losses × 3 = +6  →  +41 pts
Good luck — may your Short on the favourites pay off. ⚽
